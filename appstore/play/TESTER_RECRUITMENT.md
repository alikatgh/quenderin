# Quenderin — closed-test recruitment

Closed testing runs through a **self-serve Google Group** (Play → closed-test Alpha →
Testers = Google Groups). Anyone who joins the group becomes a tester automatically.

- **Group (owned by the Play dev account):** `quenderin-testers@googlegroups.com`
- **Join link (share this — live now, open-join):** https://groups.google.com/g/quenderin-testers
- **Play opt-in / install link:** appears on the closed-test **Testers** tab only *after* the
  app is published to the track and passes review. Once it's live, **post it into the group**
  so joiners can install.

## Why the group can be shared before the app is published
Testers can join the group immediately and start counting toward Google's **12-tester /
14-day** closed-test requirement. They just can't *install* until the build is published and
the opt-in link is posted.

## Paste-ready recruitment post (reciprocal-testing style)

> **🧠 Quenderin — private, offline AI chat (Android) — testers wanted, I'll test back!**
>
> Quenderin runs a real AI model **fully on your phone** — no account, no cloud, works
> offline after a one-time download. Looking for closed testers to help it reach production.
>
> **Join the group to become a tester:**
> https://groups.google.com/g/quenderin-testers
>
> Once you're in, I'll post the Play install link in the group. Drop your own test link and
> I'll return the favour — happy to test daily across the 14 days. 🙏

## Tester flow (what a joiner does)
1. Open the group join link → **Join group** (auto-approved; "Anyone can join").
2. After the app is published, open the **Play opt-in link** (posted in the group) → **Become a tester**.
3. Install from Play; open the app a few times across the 14 days (Play measures engagement).
