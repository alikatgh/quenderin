Last updated: 7 June 2026

The short version. Quenderin is built so there is almost nothing to collect. The app runs entirely on your device and sends your conversations to no one. 2. The app: on-device, zero collection

The Quenderin application performs all AI inference locally on your device using a model you download once. After that one-time download:

  • Your prompts, conversations, and any files you use are processed only on your device and are never transmitted to us or any third party.
  • We operate no servers that receive your content. There are no accounts, no sign-in, and no cloud sync.
  • We collect no usage analytics, no telemetry, and no crash data from the app.

For app-store disclosures, our "data collected" is None. The model file is downloaded from a public model host (e.g. Hugging Face) over a standard request; that host may log the request per its own policy. We do not receive that data.

3. This website

This marketing website is a static site that loads no third-party resources, cookies, fonts, analytics, or trackers. We do not set cookies and we do not profile visitors. Standard, transient server logs may be kept by our hosting provider for security and operation (e.g. IP address, time, page requested); we do not use these to identify you.

5. Your rights

Under the GDPR/UK GDPR you have the right to: access your data; rectify it; erase it; restrict or object to processing; data portability; and to withdraw consent at any time. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority.

Under the CCPA/CPRA, California residents have the right to know, delete, correct, and opt out of "sale"/"sharing" of personal information. We do not sell or share personal information.

To exercise any right, contact us at albert.chromium@gmail.com. Because the app holds your data only on your own device, you control that data directly — deleting the app removes it.
